# Ideation Templates

Standardized templates for brainstorming and design sessions.

## 1. Problem Framing Template
- **Problem Statement**: Clear and concise description of the challenge.
- **Goals**: What success looks like.
- **Non-Goals**: What is explicitly out of scope.
- **Constraints**: Technical, budgetary, or temporal limits.

## 2. Approach Description Template
- **Title**: Descriptive name of the approach.
- **Concept**: High-level explanation of the logic.
- **Key Components**: Main technologies or modules involved.
- **Workflow**: Step-by-step execution flow (Mermaid recommended).

## 3. Decision Log Template
- **Date**: YYYY-MM-DD.
- **Option Selected**: [Title].
- **Rationale**: Why this option was chosen over others.
- **Risks**: Potential downsides and mitigation strategies.
